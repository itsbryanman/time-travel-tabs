Time Machine Tabs - Policy

Welcome to the Time Machine Tabs Policy page. Below you'll find the details of how our extension operates, collects data, and ensures user privacy.

Purpose

The Time Machine Tabs extension enhances your browsing experience by redirecting new tabs to a random archived snapshot of a trusted news site. It does not interfere with your browsing activity or collect any unnecessary information.

Data Collection

  • No personal data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.
  • API Requests: The extension fetches data from the Wayback Machine API to provide archived snapshots.
  • Local Storage: Minimal configuration data (like preferred sites or date ranges) may be stored locally on your browser to enhance your experience.

Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • tabs: To detect when a new tab is opened and apply the extension's functionality.
  • storage: To store and retrieve user preferences locally.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to reach out to us via the GitHub repository.
